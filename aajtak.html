Who is Saveera Parkash, first Pakistani Hindu woman to contest general elections next year?

For the first time in Pakistan’s political history, a Hindu woman from Pakistan’s north-west province, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, is slated to contest the general elections next year. Saveera Parkash, a medical doctor by profession, whose family has deep connections with the Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP), has filed her nomination for the general seat of PK-25. She would contest from the Buner district in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.
